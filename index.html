About Us

We believe the first impression is the best impression. So, what better first impression than a small but concise introduction about our team!

Team Members

Our team members are Jonathan Vijay Philips, Lovnish Julka, Rushil Jain, Elroy Wilson, Gerard Joshi and Aum Yogesh. We students of The Indian High School are very enthusiastic about this project and have given it our very best!

Skill Set

Our team consists of Arduino Developers (for the Arduino board), Web Developers (for this website!), App Developers (to control the tow bar of our car using voice control), Finance Mananger, Resource Manager and Communications Manager. This helps us cover every aspect of a perfect team.

Passion

Just like our name, we are passionate about the future of automobile technology, and how it can be improved further on to create a 'magic carpet' feeling for a car owner.

Our Aim

We aim to make a structurally surable yet leightweight and feature packed car which would help all people in all walks of life. We have features like voice - controlled towing, ultrasonic sensors to detect collisions and make a beeping sound, tilt sensor which gives a beeping sound if car exceeds tilt by a ceratin angle.
